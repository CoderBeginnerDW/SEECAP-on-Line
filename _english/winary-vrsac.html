BUSINESS OPPORTUNITIES

FOR SALE
AN AWARD-WINNING WINERY WITH ITS OWN VINEYARD AND A RESTAURANT

An award-winning winery with its own production facilities and a 2,2ha vineyard all based in the famous Vrsac Vineyards region. A perfect buy for business people who are not involved in wine production and who want to start it, as a hobby or as a business venture, for retirees as a preoccupation during retirement days, diaspora people returning to the country and wanting to start something new, profitable and interesting in Serbia. No significant investment capital is required, there is a possibility for expansion of both the winery and the vineyard with generous subsidies and financial assistance from the Serbian Government. The Government provides subsidies of up to €700,000 for planting new vineyards and up to €1 million for expanding wine production capacity.

Location: The famous Vrsac Vineyards region, where the grape growing tradition dates to the Roman times, on a stretch called "Pavlicko brdo". One of the best all day long sunny vineyard position, with gentle breezes coming from the neighbouring hills, which quickly dries the leaves and fruits after the rain so that no leaves and fruits rot. In the close vicinity is located the Winery called "Vrsac vineyards".
The Winery itself is on the road between Vrsac and the Mesic Monastery, which is a local tourist mecca that is visited daily by many tourists. All tourist tours and buses pass by the Winery creating a huge opportunity for wine and hospitality tourism.

Type of land: agricultural, vineyard.

Total land area: 28.732m2

The Vineyard: An excellent location and equally good soil are a precondition for growing good quality grape as a precondition for a quality wine production with the specific terroir of the Vrsac Vineyard regions. Vineyard is in a single piece of 22.566m2. It was planted in 2002. And it is a mixture of 1.900 vines of Frankovka (Blaufränkisch) for which Vrsac Wine Region is famous, 1.000 vines of Pinot Noir, 600 vines of Muscat Ottonel and 2.300 vines of Italian Riesling and Rhine Riesling.
The size and capacity of the vineyard can be enlarged by adding adjacent land for vineyards of about 10 hectares with very little additional costs. The Government of Serbia provides subsidies of up to €700,000 for planting new vineyards and up to €1 million for expanding wine production capacity.

The Winery and the restaurant: 491m2. The Winery’s production facilities are completed and operational, the restaurant and other facilities are built but not competed. The buyer/new owner will have a chance to compete the interior to his own taste or indeed, to enlarge the current building by adding more space (accommodation, outside terrace etc).
The production capacity of the Winery is 60-70.000 bottles, the current capacity of the wine storage tanks is 24.700 litres. The Winery has all necessary equipment for receiving and processing grape and wine production, storage, bottling, labelling and packaging. All equipment is in good working order and there is no need for additional capital investment in production facility.

Legal details: A limited liability company. It has all the necessary licenses and registrations for the carrying on the business. The building has a building permit.

Main business activities: Production of grapes, wine and distillate products, hospitality

Important: The property has no mortgages, liens or other encumbrances. The company has no bank loans or other financial liabilities. There is a large finished product inventory, a wine stock worth ~€180.000 which is included in the purchase price. The company operates at a profit.

Sale: COMPLETE BUSINESS, the entire company, 100% share capital, all its assets (vineyard, winery, equipment, buildings, wine stock)

Other: On request we will send you a sales prospectus with all details of the company, its business and assets

Price: €395.000
