CORPORATE FINANCE

SEECAP offers a range of corporate finance services from a variety of sources, debt or equity.

Debt finance

  • Local or cross border loans and credits
  • Finance by the international financial institutions such as EBRD, IFC, KfW
  • Credit lines and special purpose investment credits
  • Debt securities issuance such as commercial papers, bonds
  • Finance with collateralized debt obligation CDO
  • Loan work out and corporate debt restructuring
  • Debt-to-equity swaps ana debt buy-back
  • Loan extensions, rescheduling and reprogramming
  • Access to and cooperation with EBRD, IFC and other international financial institutions
  • Transaction structuring and support throughout

Non banking sources of finance—mezzanine and private equity

  • Capital increase (capitalisation) strategy preparation
  • Growth capital
  • Business plan preparation
  • Valuation
  • Due diligence analysis
  • Drafting teasers, offering memorandum, prospectus and other documentation
  • Identification of potential regional or global private equity, mezzanine or hedge funds and other investors and contact with them
  • Marketing transaction to the investors
  • Investor selection, negotiation and support to successful conclusion
  • Other support and advise until successful project outcome
