ABOUT OUR INVESTMENT PROPERTIES OFFERS

We are first and foremost a financial consultancy. From time to time our corporate clients ask us to identify potential buyers for assets from their real estate asset portfolio. Some of such properties are presented below. These are all investment properties, for further investment and development or for rental and/or resale.

Before listing such properties, we do our best to carry out a proper due diligence of the asset. In addition, we use our knowledge and experience to create [financial] vision of the investment project by analysing such data as development costs, market conditions, urban planing and legal requirements etc to assist the prospective buyer in his decision making process. For the majority of the presented investment projects we have prepared a project or investment study as a separate document which we send to a prospective purchaser for his consideration. This is a notable benefit of buying an investment properties through this firm.

In addition, we can assist buyers with

  • raising debt and equity finance for the project completion from local or international sources of finance
  • preparation of feasibility studies
  • further financial analysis and financial modelling of the future project
  • financial management of the project
  • and other services as elaborated here

We have a relationship with external suppliers of goods and services which we could make available to the buyer such as:

  • architects and designers
  • urban planners
  • lawyers
  • construction companies
  • construction material suppliers and similar
